Philippine Society of Mechanical Engineers

Hi there, we are PSME

A dynamic hub for mechanical engineering professionals, offering expertise, publications, events, and resources to propel careers.

Founded in 1952, we are a premier organization with:

  • - 104,000 Licensed Mechanical Engineers and Certified Plant Mechanics
  • - 7,000+ Professional Mechanical Engineers
  • - 87 Chapters across the Philippines and Worldwide

Engage with us through our various conventions, conferences, and seminars:

  • - 63rd National Convention
  • - Philippine Machinery Exhibition
  • - Regional Conferences
  • - Mechanical Engineering Educators Congress
  • - National Student Conference

Join us for Professional Development Courses and access over 45 Technical Sessions in our National Convention. Prioritizing user experience, we're here to connect, educate, and advance.
